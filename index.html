Biography []

I am currently a fitst-year Ph.D. student at the College of Information Science and Techonlogy, Fudan University, supervised by Prof. Xiang Li and Prof. Cong Li.

I am mainly focusing on improving the generalization and stability of machine learning systems in the open, dynamic, and uncertain real world. My current research interests include domain generalization/adaptation and causal inference.
